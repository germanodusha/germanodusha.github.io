Germano Dushá (1989) is a curator ↭ writer ↭ critic ↭ cultural agent
with a law degree (FGV Direito SP) and a postgraduate degree in
Art: Criticism and Curatorship (PUC-SP), working mainly with curatorial,
literature, and hypermedia experiments. In the intersection of
aesthetics, critical thought and esoteric traditions, his practice assumes
multiple forms to investigate social imaginaries, and dwell on the energy
fluxes connected to radical subjective experiences and transmutation processes.
✸ Born in Serra dos Carajás
⌂ Based in São Paulo
✉ germano@ofora.org
